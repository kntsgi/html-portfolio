Brian's Favorites

My top 3 movies of all-time.

I. Big Hero 6

This movie tells the tragic, yet heart-warming story of an unlikely hero's journey with an unexpected friend. While wrestling with his own sense of morality, the young hero must learn to use his newly found power

II. Suzume

A beautifully drawn and animated masterpiece that tells a fantastical and heart-wrenching tale. Centered on a young girl named Suzume, who is thrown into a chaotic world, the story follows her as she is forced to adapt to an unknown, chaotic world in order to survive and prevent wide-spread calamity.

III. Spider-Man: Across the Spider-verse

Following the success of "Into the Spider-verse," Sony releases another hit animated adventure centered on the young Miles Morales as he discovers the secrets of the multi-verse
